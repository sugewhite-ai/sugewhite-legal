Privacy Policy — Sugewhite Auto Clip

Last updated: April 29, 2026

1. Overview

Sugewhite Auto Clip is a private automation tool used exclusively by content creator Sugewhite to upload gaming clips to TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles data.

2. Data Collection

This App does not collect any personal data from TikTok users or viewers. The App only accesses the TikTok account of its owner (Sugewhite) for the purpose of posting content.

3. Data Usage

The only data accessed by this App is:

  • TikTok account credentials of the App owner
  • Video files created by the App owner for upload

This data is used solely to automate content posting and is never shared with third parties.

4. Data Storage

No user data is stored by this App. Video files are stored on the App owner's private server (Synology NAS) and deleted or archived after upload.

5. Third Party Services

This App uses the TikTok API in compliance with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

This App does not collect data from anyone, including minors.

7. Contact

For privacy questions contact: sugewhite12345@gmail.com
